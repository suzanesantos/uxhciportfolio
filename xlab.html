X Lab A


3D Assets for AR Mobile Applications


These artworks are part of the self-chosen assignment of the course X Lab A. For this assignment, I decided to learn how to create 3D models, which could be used in augmented reality mobile interactions. The tools used through this learning process were Blender and Vectary. To learn them, I followed some tutorials on Youtube.


Part of the displayed models covers some inherent concept of 3D design, such as Modeling, Sculping, Shading and UV Mapping.



  • The Couch

    • The IKEA Place app inspired me to design this 3D couch. Based on that, I decided to replicate the experience by creating this furniture and provide the visualization of it through a mobile device.




    Bellow some steps of the process can be seen.


    Modeling

    Creating the model shape.




    Sculping

    Inflating and provide shape to the cushions.




    UV Mapping

    defining the limit of the faces to place the material on the face.




    Materials

    Applying material with shaders



    Applying material in Vectary, as it gets a better result when exporting to AR application.
